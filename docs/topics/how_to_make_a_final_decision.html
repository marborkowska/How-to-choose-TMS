Decision-making process

The TMS market is often confusing and still evolving. That's why the decision to buy a new system or change an existing one is not easy.

You have decided to purchase a TMS, but are still wondering how to start the whole process. First, you need to outline your needs and goals to be achieved.
To choose the right TMS for your company, you should consider a lot of different aspects, both from business and technical perspectives. To make it easier for you, I strongly encourage you to follow these steps:
  1. Analyzing your translation process
  2. Researching the TMS market
  3. Budgeting
Note: The whole process can take even a few months so be prepared to allocate a specific period of time. Don't hurry up - the decision is important and costly! I also recommend to set yourself a go-live date early and try to stick to it.

In the end, what you are looking for is:

  • higher team productivity
  • more effective collaboration
  • faster time to market
  • reduced translation costs
  • return on your investment
